Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The short version: We do not store, sell or share your text. What you type stays private.

What Data We Collect

ParaFree does not collect personal data. No account, no signup and no email address is required to use any feature of this service.

When you submit text for paraphrasing, humanizing, summarizing or any other tool, that text is sent directly to an AI engine for processing. It is never stored on our servers and is immediately discarded after the result is returned to you.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand anonymous traffic patterns — things like which pages are visited and which countries users come from. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google AdSense

ParaFree uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Cookies

ParaFree itself does not set any cookies. The only cookies present on this site are from Google Analytics and Google AdSense, as described above. These are standard third-party cookies used across millions of websites.

No Account Required

ParaFree never requires you to create an account, provide an email address, or sign in. There is no user database. We cannot identify you.

Data Security

Because we do not store your text or personal data, there is nothing to breach. Your submitted text exists only long enough to be processed by the AI engine and returned as a result.

Third-Party AI Engines

Your text is processed by third-party AI providers including Groq, Google Gemini, Cerebras, Mistral and others. These providers have their own privacy policies which govern how they handle API requests. We recommend not submitting confidential, sensitive or personally identifying information through any AI tool.

Children's Privacy

ParaFree is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email us at parafree.tool@gmail.com.
