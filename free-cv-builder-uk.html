UK CV Format Guide

How to Write a UK CV in 2026

UK CV conventions are specific. Here's exactly what British employers expect.

Length
Maximum 2 Pages

A UK CV should be a maximum of 2 pages. UK recruiters and employers expect a concise, well-structured document. If you're a recent graduate, 1 page is acceptable.

Personal Statement
3–4 Sentences at Top

UK CVs begin with a personal statement (not a career objective). This is a brief paragraph summarising your experience, key skills and what you bring to the role.

Photo
No Photo ✗

Never include a photo, date of birth or age on a UK CV. This is to comply with UK equality legislation. It also applies to marital status and nationality.

Spelling
British English

Use British English spelling: colour, organisation, behaviour, programme, analyse, licence (noun), specialise. Avoid American spellings — UK employers notice this.

References
"Available on Request"

UK CVs do not typically list references. Simply write "References available on request" at the end of your CV. Unlike Australian CVs, you don't need to name referees upfront.

Hobbies & Interests
Optional but Useful

UK CVs often include a brief hobbies section. Keep it to 2-3 lines and focus on interests that demonstrate relevant skills: leadership, teamwork, creativity or analytical thinking.

Frequently Asked Questions

UK CV Questions Answered

What is the standard UK CV format? +
A UK CV should be a maximum of 2 pages. Include a personal statement at the top, work experience in reverse chronological order, education, skills, and "References available on request." Do not include a photo, date of birth, age or marital status.
Can international students use this CV builder in the UK? +
Yes. ParaFree is used by international students at UCL, Imperial College, University of Manchester, University of Edinburgh, King's College London and many more. The builder supports the Graduate Route visa work rights section.
Should I include a photo on my UK CV? +
No. Do not include a photo, date of birth or age on a UK CV. This is to comply with UK equality legislation and prevent unconscious bias in recruitment. UK CVs should only include professional and academic information.
How long should a UK CV be? +
A UK CV should be a maximum of 2 pages. Employers and recruiters in the UK expect a concise, focused CV. Recent graduates may have a 1-2 page CV. Unlike Australian CVs (2-3 pages), UK CVs are expected to be shorter.
Can I apply for graduate schemes with ParaFree CV builder? +
Yes. ParaFree's templates are suitable for Big 4 graduate schemes (Deloitte, PwC, KPMG, EY), NHS Graduate Management Training, Civil Service Fast Stream and all major UK employer graduate programmes. The ATS-friendly Classic template is recommended.
What is the Graduate Route visa and how do I mention it on my UK CV? +
The Graduate Route visa allows international students who have completed a UK degree to stay and work in the UK for 2 years (3 years for PhD graduates). You can note this on your CV as: "Work Authorisation: Graduate Route Visa — unrestricted work rights until [date]."
