Frequently Asked Questions

What is the AI Humanizer tool?
The AI Humanizer takes text written by AI (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini etc.) and rewrites it to sound naturally human. It reduces AI patterns that detectors like GPTZero and Turnitin AI look for.
Will humanized text bypass Turnitin AI detection?
Our humanizer significantly reduces AI detection scores by rewriting text with natural human patterns, varied sentence structure, and organic flow. Results vary — we always recommend reviewing and adding your own personal touches after humanizing.
Is ParaFree really free with no word limit?
Yes — completely free, forever. We use multiple AI engines so when one reaches its daily limit, another takes over automatically. You always get results.
Do I need to create an account?
No account, no signup, no email required. Just paste your text and click. That's it.
How is this different from Quillbot?
Quillbot limits free users to 125 words and charges for more. ParaFree has no word limit, includes a free AI Humanizer, and is completely free — powered by 6 AI engines working together.
Is my text stored or shared?
No. Your text is sent directly to the AI engine for processing and is never stored on our servers. Your privacy is safe.
