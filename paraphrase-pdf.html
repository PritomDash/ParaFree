Scanned PDF Support

Works with Scanned PDFs Too

Most paraphrasers fail on scanned PDFs because they can't read image-based text. ParaFree uses OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to extract text from the images first, then paraphrases it.

🔍 How OCR works on ParaFree:
  1. PDF is loaded page by page
  2. Each page is rendered as a high-resolution image
  3. OCR reads the text from each image
  4. Recognized text is sent to the AI for paraphrasing
  5. Result is downloaded as PDF or Word

OCR accuracy depends on scan quality. Clear, high-contrast scans produce best results.

💡 For Best Results

When to Use DOCX Instead of PDF

PDF format doesn't store text the same way as DOCX. This means some formatting (exact fonts, colors, complex layouts) can't be fully preserved when downloading a paraphrased PDF.

Choose based on your goal:
✓
Use PDF upload when:
You only need the paraphrased text content and basic layout.
✓
Use DOCX upload when:
You need 100% format preservation — fonts, colors, bold and tables exactly preserved.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I paraphrase a PDF file online free?

Yes. Upload any PDF to ParaFree and AI will paraphrase the text content. Download as PDF or Word. Completely free, no signup required.

Does it work with scanned PDFs?

Yes. ParaFree uses OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to read text from scanned or image-based PDFs before paraphrasing. This works even if the PDF has no selectable text — as long as the scan is reasonably clear.

Will my PDF formatting be preserved?

PDF formatting is partially preserved. For complete formatting preservation (fonts, colors, tables), we recommend uploading your document as .docx instead — ParaFree preserves DOCX formatting perfectly. PDF is best when you only need the paraphrased text content.

Can I download as Word after paraphrasing a PDF?

Yes. After paraphrasing a PDF, you can download the result as a .docx Word document or as PDF. Both options are completely free.

Is there a page limit?

No. Paraphrase PDFs of any length completely free. Multi-page documents are fully supported without restrictions or payment.

My PDF has images — will those be affected?

No. Images embedded in your PDF are not affected by the paraphrasing process. Only text content is extracted and rewritten.
