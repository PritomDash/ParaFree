Can I paraphrase a PowerPoint online free?

Yes. ParaFree is the only free tool that paraphrases PPTX files. Upload your presentation, AI rewrites the text, and you download it with all design intact. No signup, no payment needed.

Will my slides' design change?

No. We only replace text inside text boxes. Images, backgrounds, animations, fonts, colours and layouts stay exactly the same. Your presentation looks identical — just with different words.

Is there a slide limit?

No limits. Paraphrase presentations of any length completely free. Whether you have 5 slides or 100, ParaFree handles it without charging you.

Do I need to sign up?

No signup, no email, no credit card. Head to the ParaFree homepage, upload your PPTX, and paraphrase instantly. Nothing is saved to your account because there is no account.

Does it work with Google Slides?

Yes, indirectly. Export your Google Slides as .pptx (File → Download → Microsoft PowerPoint .pptx), paraphrase it on ParaFree, then re-upload to Google Slides via File → Import Slides.

What languages does the paraphraser support?

ParaFree supports 10 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Italian, Dutch, Arabic, Hindi and more. The AI paraphrases in the same language as your slides.
