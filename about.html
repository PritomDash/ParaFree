About ParaFree

A free AI writing suite built for students, writers and professionals everywhere — with no word limits, no signup, and no hidden costs.

Our Mission

Premium writing tools shouldn't cost money. That's the idea behind ParaFree. We built this platform because too many students — especially international students studying in Australia, the UK, the USA, Singapore and across Europe — were being charged for basic writing tools that should be free.

Quillbot charges. Grammarly charges. Every decent paraphrasing tool has a paywall. ParaFree doesn't — and it never will. Our mission is to make high-quality AI writing tools genuinely accessible to everyone, regardless of budget.

How It Stays Free

ParaFree runs on 6 AI engines working together in a smart fallback chain — Groq, Gemini, Cerebras, OpenRouter, Mistral and Cloudflare. When one engine hits its rate limit, the next one takes over automatically. This is why ParaFree almost never goes down, even during peak hours.

The site is supported by Google AdSense advertising, which keeps everything completely free for users. No subscriptions, no paywalls, no accounts.

Who Uses ParaFree

ParaFree is used daily by students, academics, content writers, ESL learners and professionals across the world. Our users are based in Australia, the United States, the United Kingdom, Singapore, Canada, India, the Philippines, and many more countries. We support 10 languages so non-English speakers can use every tool in their own language.

ParaFree is and always will be 100% free — no signup, no credit card, no word limit. That's a promise.
