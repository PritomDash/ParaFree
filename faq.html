Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ParaFree.

Is ParaFree really free forever?
Yes. ParaFree is completely free with no hidden costs, no subscription tiers and no paywall. The service is supported by Google AdSense advertising, which allows us to keep every feature free for all users indefinitely.
Is there a word limit?
No — there is no word limit at all. You can paraphrase entire essays, research papers, full articles or long documents in a single request. This is one of the main things that sets ParaFree apart from tools like Quillbot, which limit free users to 125 words.
Do I need to sign up or create an account?
No signup, no account, no email required — ever. Just go to parafree.app, paste your text and click the button. There is no registration process and we never ask for personal information.
How does the AI Humanizer work?
The AI Humanizer takes text that was written by an AI (such as ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini or any other tool) and rewrites it using natural human patterns — varied sentence length, organic word choice, conversational phrasing and authentic flow. This significantly reduces the AI detection scores produced by tools like GPTZero, Winston AI and Turnitin's AI detector.
Will it bypass Turnitin and GPTZero?
Our AI Humanizer is designed to significantly reduce detection scores. Results vary depending on how much of the text was originally AI-generated and which detection tool is being used. For best results, we recommend reviewing the humanized output and adding your own personal voice, examples and edits. No tool can guarantee 100% bypass of AI detection.
What is the PPTX Paraphraser?
The PPTX Paraphraser lets you upload a PowerPoint (.pptx) file. ParaFree reads every text box on every slide, paraphrases the content using AI, and lets you download a new PPTX file with all the original formatting preserved — only the text is changed. It works with presentations of any size, with no slide limit.
Which languages are supported?
ParaFree supports 10 languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Italian, Dutch, Polish, Turkish and Indonesian. You can select your language from the dropdown before processing, and all tools including the paraphraser, humanizer and summarizer will work in that language.
Is my text stored or shared?
No. Your text is sent to an AI engine for processing and is immediately discarded once the result is returned. We do not store, log or sell your text. We do not have a user database. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How is ParaFree different from Quillbot?
Quillbot's free plan limits you to 125 words per paraphrase and charges $19.95/month for unlimited access. ParaFree has no word limit, is completely free, and also includes an AI Humanizer, Summarizer, Grammar Checker, AI Detector and PPTX Paraphraser — all free, all unlimited, no account needed.
What AI engines does ParaFree use?
ParaFree uses 6 AI engines in a smart fallback chain: Groq, Google Gemini, Cerebras, OpenRouter, Mistral and Cloudflare. When the primary engine (Groq) is busy or rate-limited, the system automatically tries the next one. This is why ParaFree almost never goes down — there are always backup engines ready.
What file types can I upload?
ParaFree supports TXT files, PDF files, DOCX (Word) files and PPTX (PowerPoint) files. For PPTX files, use the PPTX mode in the Paraphraser tab. For other file types, you can upload them to paste the text into the text area for processing.
Can I download my results?
Yes. After processing, you can download results as a PDF or DOCX (Word) file. For PPTX files, you can download the paraphrased presentation as a new .pptx file with the original formatting preserved. You can also copy text to clipboard with one click.
How do I paraphrase a PowerPoint presentation?
Go to parafree.app, click the Paraphraser tab, then click the upload icon and select your .pptx file. ParaFree will load the file and switch to PPTX mode. Select your paraphrase style and language, then click Paraphrase. The AI will process your slides in batches and you can download the result when finished.
Is it safe to use for university assignments?
ParaFree is a writing assistance tool. Whether using it complies with your institution's academic integrity policy depends on your specific university's rules and how you use the output. Always review, edit and personalise any AI-assisted work before submission, and familiarise yourself with your institution's policies. ParaFree does not endorse academic dishonesty.
What if all AI engines are busy?
ParaFree uses 6 AI engines in a fallback chain. If all engines are simultaneously rate-limited or unavailable, you'll see an error message asking you to try again in a moment. This is rare and usually resolves within a few minutes. The AI status indicators in the tool show you which engines are currently active (green), at their limit (orange) or unavailable (red).
