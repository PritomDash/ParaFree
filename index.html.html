Frequently Asked Questions

Is ParaFree really free with no word limit?
Yes — completely free, forever. We use multiple AI engines so when one reaches its daily limit, another takes over automatically. You always get results.
Do I need to create an account?
No account, no signup, no email required. Just paste your text and click Paraphrase. That's it.
How is this different from Quillbot?
Quillbot limits free users to 125 words and charges for more. ParaFree has no word limit and is completely free — powered by multiple AI engines working together.
Is my text stored or shared?
No. Your text is sent directly to the AI engine for processing and is never stored on our servers. Your privacy is safe.
What if it says "try again later"?
Very rarely all 4 AI engines hit their daily free limits at the same time. This resets every 24 hours — just come back in a few hours and it will work again.
